Step 3：通过听写+跟读，彻底吃透文章内容

听写练习

请参考 怎样吃透一篇托福听力文章 中的要求完成听写练习。完成之后比对原文（见下方），弄清楚自己错听的原因。如果时间实在特别紧张，无法完成听写，那么请务必完成下面的跟读练习。


跟读练习

在这部分，我带着大家逐句分析所有造成听力障碍的语音现象。强烈推荐所有同学都能一起张嘴跟着练习。只有熟练掌握美式英语的发音规则、语调上的重点，才能让我们在听的过程中更加有的放矢，更轻松。


下面是标出语音现象的文本，请在练习中使用。橙色是考点句。

逐句跟读音频+文本

（在这部分，我尽可能保留了原文完整的句子，所以有些句子会比较长，也是为了让大家练习听一个完整意群的能力。如果有困难可以自己在中间断一下。）


1. - Hi. Is um Nathan around? 

- No. Sorry. He's gone for the day. Can I help you with something?


2. Well I hope so. See, I'm supposed to start volunteering as a tour guide on Monday. I'm scheduled（安排） to give a tour at 10.

3. I did my first volunteer training session (培训课程) with Nathan the other day and I was supposed to have the second session tomorrow afternoon, which is the last one you guys offer this week. Right?

4. Yes it is, in fact, it's the last one we have scheduled (安排) for this semester. Yeah, that's what I was afraid of.

5. So I don't know what to do because I'm signed up (报名) to give that tour on Monday, and it turns out I have to make up a test (补考) tomorrow afternoon.

6. Hmm, well yes, that is a problem, I'm afraid. Because we do require two training sessions before you're allowed to start giving tours.

7. I know. I was hoping maybe I could work something out with Nathan.

8.  He uh he gave us his home number at the original training session, but I'm not sure where I put it. Could you possibly give it to me again?

9. Well, no, I'm not allowed to give out employees’ phone numbers. It wouldn't do you any good anyway. Nathan can't waive (放弃) that training requirement anymore than I can. 

10.  But I can sign you up for the first session next semester and you could start giving tours then.

11.  That's too late. Oh, not that I wouldn't want to do tours next semester. I mean I do wanna keep doing it. It's just that…

12.  See, I really need to start this semester. It's…my art history professor is giving me a chance to bring my grade up.


13.  He said I could get extra credit if I wrote a paper for him and volunteered here at the art museum.

14. -Is this professor Tyler’s class? -Hmm.

-Yeah, I thought so. You’re not the first student he sent our way.


15.  - Look, did he specify that it had to be as a tour guide? 

- Well, not exactly, I just thought... 

- Because believe me, we could use you in other capacities.

16. - Really? Like… 

- Well, it's not as glamorous as being a tour guide, but we always need people to work in a museum gift shop.

17.   - Or how are you at data entry? Data processing, stuff like that?

- Um pretty good actually.

18.  Cuz we desperately need someone right away to help us keep our membership lists up to date, work on our monthly newsletter, things like that.

19. -Sounds kind of boring, I know. But after you've had some experience, it could lead to a paid position.

- Well, that’d certainly be a plus.

20. -Tell you what, let me just check with professor Tyler, make sure it's all right with him, and I’ll get back to you sometime tomorrow. Okay?

- Okay, great.
