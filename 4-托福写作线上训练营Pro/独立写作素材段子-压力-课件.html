英文内容：


It is high time that governments should arouse people’s awareness of the importance of physical soundness. As is known to all, along with the acceleration of social development, people, especially young people, are confronted with unprecedentedly fierce competition. They need to work very hard to guarantee a decent life, which causes them way heavier pressure than before. Under such circumstances, most of them are prone to be caught in a sub-health state.


Because it is sort of governments’ responsibilities to improve citizens’ health condition, governments should increase access to public exercise equipment in the neighborhood and raise people’s awareness of the importance of doing exercise regularly through the mass media.


中文翻译：


政府是时候提高人们关于身体健康重要性的认识了。众所周知，随着社会发展进程的加快，人们，尤其是年轻人，正面临着前所未有的激烈竞争。他们需要非常努力的工作来保证体面的生活，这使得他们面临着比以前更为沉重的压力。在这样的情况下，他们中的大多数都呈现出亚健康的状态。


因为政府的责任之一是改善居民的健康状况，所以政府应该增加社区内公共健身设施的获取并通过大众传媒提升人们对于规律运动的重要性的认识。

  • decent adj. 体面的

  • unprecedentedly adv. 前所未有地
