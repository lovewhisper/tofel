英文内容：


Living in a big city has several benefits, especially for those young people who want to strive for better career development.


First, there are more job opportunities readily available in a big city than in a small town. Moreover, not merely are there more job positions, the quality of the positions is also much higher in a big city. In addition, the average payment is more decent. 


Second, children growing in urban areas are more likely to receive an education of higher quality compared with their counterparts living in a small town. 


Finally, the living standard in a big city is usually superior to that in a small town. In big cities, there are more commodities and services on the market, more available public facilities, and even many more ways of entertainment. 


中文翻译：


住在大城市里有一些好处，尤其是对于那些想通过奋斗获得更好职业发展的年轻人来说。


首先，相较于小的城镇，大城市有更多可触手可及的工作机会。此外，不仅工作的岗位更多，这些岗位的质量在大的城市里面也更高。而且，平均的薪资也更丰厚。


其次，在城市成长的儿童相较于居住在小城镇的同龄人来说更有可能接受更高质量的教育。


最后，大城市的生活质量通常优于小城镇。在大城市里，市场上有更多的商品和服务，更多可用的公共设施，甚至是更多的娱乐方式。



语言表达整理


  • strive for ≈ struggle for = trying so hard to get ...

  • receive an education of higher quality ... 获得质量更高的教育 

  • counterparts ... 竞争对⼿手

  • be superior to ... 超过... 优于...
