英文内容：


According to the psychologist Abraham Maslow’s theory of "Hierarchy of Needs", people’s actions are motivated to fulfill basic needs before moving on to other, more advanced needs. In the pyramid in which form the hierarchy is displayed, the lowest level comprises of basic physical requirements such as the need for food and shelter; higher up is the need for safety and security. As people progress up the pyramid, needs become increasingly psychological and social. Soon, the need for love, friendship and intimacy becomes important, followed by the need for personal esteem and feelings of accomplishment. The need which tops the list is self-actualization, which is a process of growing and developing as a person in order to achieve individual potential (to be all that we can be). 


中文翻译：


根据心理学家亚拉伯罕·马斯洛的”需求等级“理论，在转向其他的更高等级的需求之前，人们的行为被驱动来实现基本的需求。在需求等级的金字塔上，最底层是由基本的生理需求，比如对于食物和居所的需求，所组成的；较高的是对于安全和安稳的需求。当人们往金字塔上端前进的时候，需求变得更加的心理化和社会化。很快，对于爱，友谊和亲密关系的需求变得更加重要，紧随其后的是对于自尊和成就的需求。在顶端的是自我实现的需求，这是一个生而为人，为获得个人潜能从而成为所有能成为的人的不断成长和发展的过程。
