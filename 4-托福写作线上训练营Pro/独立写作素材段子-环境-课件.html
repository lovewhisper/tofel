英文内容：


First, environmental protection is closely bound up with the health of every single person in this world. If the environment is polluted, the basic elements people need to survive, such as water and air, may be affected, and thus disastrous results may occur. Recently I have read an appalling story from newspapers that there is a village in the center of China where the majority of its residents, including kids and seniors, suffer from cancers such as lung cancer, stomach cancer and lymph cancer. The investigators found that the villagers’ drinking water was from a river severely contaminated by the industrial discharges of enterprises located at the upstream. 


In addition, environmental protection is conducive to the sustainability of economic development. There are some idioms in China, “killing the hens for the egg”, “draining the pond for the fish”, and “burning the woods for the prey”. These words of ancient wisdom warn modern people not to gain profits without thinking of the future. In other words, we cannot simply satisfy the needs of contemporary people regardless of the interest of our next generation. Imagine that if we were in excessive pursuit of economic development by consuming all the natural resources, hunting for precious animals mercilessly and cutting down forests, what would become of our descendants? 


中文翻译：


首先，环境保护和世界上每一个个体的健康息息相关。如果环境被污染了，人们赖以生存的基本元素，比如说水和空气，可能会被影响，因此可能会发生灾难性的后果，最近我在报纸上读到了一篇骇人听闻的文章，在中国中部有一个村庄，大部分的村民，包括孩子和老人，饱受包括肺癌，胃癌以及淋巴癌等癌症的摧残。调查表明村子的饮用水来自于被上游企业工业排放物严重污染的河流。


此外，环境保护对于经济的可持续发展也至关重要。中国有一些谚语：杀鸡取卵，竭泽而渔，焚林而猎。这些充满古代智慧的话语警告现代人不要忽视将来去获取利益。换句话说，我们不能仅仅满足现代人的需求而无视下一代的利益。想想一下如果我们通过消耗所有的自然资源，毫无人性地不傻珍惜动物以及砍伐森林来过度追求经济的发展，我们的后代会变成什么样子？
