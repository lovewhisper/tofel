英文内容：


Teamwork, which is greatly valued in modern world, is the key to career success. Only through interactions with partners can one master the art of cooperation, which vividly demonstrates the significance of having a good relationship with others. At school, students are assigned many projects or group tasks. The success of any project requires the united efforts of all group members. During the course of a group task, each individual has to work together with his partner. Bearing a common goal in mind, each member is devoted to the assigned part of the task. Just like different components of an engine, everyone is interrelated. If a single part doesn’t work, the whole engine may stop immediately or even break down. This example is a good demonstration of the value of creating a harmonious relationship with others. 


中文翻译：


被在现代社会尤为重视的合作是事业成功的关键。人只有通过和搭档的互动才能掌握合作的艺术，这生动体现了和其他人有好的关系的重要性。在学校里，学生被分配了很多项目或者小组练习。任何一个项目的成功都需要所有组员的共同努力。在团队联系的过程中，每个个体都需要和他的搭档一起工作。基于共同的目标，每个成员都致力于被分配的那部分任务。就像是一个引擎的不同组成部分，每个人都是相关的。如果一个单独的部分出故障了，整个引擎可能会直接停下甚至崩溃。这个例子是和他人构建和谐关系的重要性的好的体现。



语言表达整理


  • the art of cooperation 合作的艺术

  • components = parts 部分/组成部分

  • interrelated 相互关联的

  • break down 发⽣故障、损坏

  • a harmonious relationship ... 和谐的关系 ...
