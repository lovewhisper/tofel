英文内容：


First of all, emphasis should be placed on what has been suggested in the famous saying:“interest is the best teacher”. Evidently, the primary step toward a person’s success is to have an emotional preference. In fact, interest constitutes the biggest impetus in learning, keeping one refreshed and preventing feelings of boredom and tiredness. Those epoch-making accomplishments in the scientific world owe much to the initial interest of the scientists. 


To illustrate, Charles Darwin is famous for his masterpiece entitled on the Origin of Species. His interest in nature led him to quit his medical studies at the University of Edinburgh and explore natural science instead. Further education at Cambridge continued to evoke his passion for natural science. At last, his enthusiasm and untiring efforts paid off. His story perfectly indicates the importance of interest in a person’s success. 


中文翻译：


首先，重点应该被放在著名谚语中说的：“兴趣是最好的老师”。很明显，一个人成功的主要步骤是有一个情感上的偏向。事实上，兴趣构成了学习中的最大动力，保持新鲜感并且防止无聊或者疲累的情绪。那些科学领域的划时代成就很大程度上归根于科学家的原始兴趣。


具体来说，查尔斯·达尔文因为他的著作《物种起源》而闻名。他对于自然的兴趣使得他放弃了在爱丁堡大学的医学学习转而探索自然科学。在剑桥的进一步教育持续激发了他对于自然科学的热情。最终，他的热情与不知疲倦的努力得到了回报。他的故事完美诠释了兴趣在一个人成功中的重要性。

  • constitute v. 组成，构成；建立；任命

  • impetus n. 动力；促进；冲力

  • epoch-making accomplishment 划时代的成就 

  • masterpiece n. 大师之作

  • pay off... 成功

  • untiring efforts 不懈的努⼒
