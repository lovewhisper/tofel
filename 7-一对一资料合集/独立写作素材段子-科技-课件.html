英文内容：

Firstly, advancement in medical technology makes it possible that most common diseases can be controlled or cured more efficiently and effectively. Along with the ever-accelerating developments of science and technology come major breakthroughs in various disciplines related to medical science like chemistry, biology and anatomy, the combination of which has been pushing medical science to a higher level. Scientists have found solutions to many diseases that used to be considered as fatal. People’s health condition, therefore, can be ameliorated and average life expectancy be prolonged. For example, smallpox, eradicated nowadays, was once the deadliest disease in human history, engulfing tens of thousands of people’s lives. But with the invention of a certain targeted vaccine, people finally put an end to this rampant epidemic. 


中文翻译：

首先，医疗科技的进步使得大部分常见疾病能够被有效控制和治愈成为了可能。随着不断加速发展的科学技术，与医药科学相关的领域比如化学，生物学和解剖学不断取得重大突破，这些领域的综合推动医疗科技到了更高的等级。科学家已经找到了很多过去被认定是致命的疾病的治疗方案。因此，人们的健康状况得意改善，平均寿命也能够被延长。举例来说，天花，如今已经被消灭了，曾经是人类历史上的致命疾病，夺去了成千上万人的生命。但是随着特定靶向疫苗的发明，人们最终终结了这个猖獗的传染病。


语言表达整理

  • ever-accelerating development 前所未有的加速发展 

  • breakthrough n. 突破

  • anatomy n. 解剖学

  • combination n. 组合、集合

  • fatal adj. 致命的 lethal / incurable

  • ameliorate v. 改善，减轻，改良

  • life expectancy ... 寿命

  • smallpox 天花

  • eradicate vt. 根除；根绝；消灭 

  • engulf v. 吞噬
